# Facial Recognition
**PROJECT BRIEF** - 
This is a short project where an AI defines your facial expression. It uses Face-API to mark landmarks on your face and detect in what position they are in. It then uses the trained AI to tell you what emotion it is. It can also tell you the estimated age and gender, as well as race. However, I have not implemented that for this project, as this is solely to detect your emotion from your expression.
**FIXES TO COMMON ISSUES** - 
If your face is not being detected, then please check your environment's lighting and make sure it is well lit. If your webcam is not showing up on the website at all, then make sure it has permission to your webcam/camera.
**WHERE TO FIND THIS APP** - To access this app, just search up adviksfacialexpression.netlify.app on your desired browser, or click this link: https://adviksfacialexpression.netlify.app/
